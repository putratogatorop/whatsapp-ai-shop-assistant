# Return & Refund Policy

- Items can be returned within 7 days of delivery if unused and in original
  packaging.
- To start a return, send us a photo of the item and your order number via
  WhatsApp and our team will confirm the return address.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-10 business days
  after we receive the returned item.
- Sale items marked "final sale" are not eligible for return.
- Defective or wrong items are eligible for a free replacement — no return
  shipping cost to the customer in that case.
