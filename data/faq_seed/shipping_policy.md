# Shipping Policy

We ship nationwide within Indonesia using JNE, J&T, and SiCepat.

- Orders placed before 3 PM WIB are processed the same business day.
- Standard shipping takes 2-4 business days for Java, 4-7 business days for
  other islands.
- Shipping cost is calculated automatically at checkout based on destination
  and package weight.
- We currently do not offer international shipping.
- Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via WhatsApp.

## Same-day delivery

Same-day delivery (Gojek/Grab instant courier) is available for customers in
Jakarta, Bandung, and Surabaya for orders placed before 12 PM WIB.
