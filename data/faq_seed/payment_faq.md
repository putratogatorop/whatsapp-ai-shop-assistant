# Payment FAQ

We accept the following payment methods:

- Bank transfer (BCA, BNI, Mandiri, BRI)
- QRIS
- E-wallet: GoPay, OVO, DANA
- Cash on Delivery (COD) for Jabodetabek addresses only

## How to pay after checkout

1. Complete your order in the chat with our assistant.
2. You will receive payment instructions with the total amount and a virtual
   account / QRIS code.
3. After paying, send a screenshot of your payment receipt in this WhatsApp
   chat. Our system automatically reads the screenshot and updates your order
   status.
4. You will get a confirmation message once payment is verified, usually
   within a few minutes during business hours.

## Payment not confirmed?

If your payment isn't confirmed within 30 minutes, reply "talk to human" and
our team will manually check it for you.
