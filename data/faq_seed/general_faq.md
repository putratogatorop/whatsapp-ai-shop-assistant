# General Store Information

Toko Maju Jaya is an online shop selling home & kitchen products, operating
100% through WhatsApp.

- Customer service hours: Monday-Saturday, 9 AM - 9 PM WIB.
- Outside business hours, our AI assistant can still answer product
  questions, check stock, and take orders — a human will only step in when
  needed (e.g. payment verification issues or complaints).
- To check an order status, tell the assistant your order number.
- To browse products, just ask the assistant what you're looking for, e.g.
  "do you have a rice cooker?"
